But did you know not all wet foods are created equal? Some are better than others, and you are your own best advocate in convincing your human to buy quality wet food!

The vet had me on a powdered probiotic that was unpleasant to the taste and showed minimal results. It wasn't until one fateful trip to the emergency vet (never be afraid to get a second opinion!) that everything changed. That kind doctor recommended Fancy Feast, and after two years of zero digestive issues, I'm here to recommend it to you!
